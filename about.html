Hello! My name is Oliver Doc. (qalaxydoq)!

I am a short 17 year old Scottish and autistic guy who loves to draw! When I am not making visual art I like to write poems, my WIP book (My Name Is Jupiter), Code (HTML) and play video games.

My favourite things to draw are dogs, robots, weird teeth and anthro animals!

I dream of going to Full Sail University to study digital art and live in NJ or PA. I don't have many goals and I haven't quite figured out exactly what I want to do for the rest of my life yet, but Full Sail University seems like a goal that isn't too much of a stretch!

I am currently doing freelance work (art commissions) as my only job until I am old enough (and capable enough) of supporting myself! If you are interested in commissioning me feel free to check them out here!
